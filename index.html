an interactive web-based platform designed to enhance emotional intelligence among students with learning disabilities

1 in 5 children in the U.S. have learning disabilities

... they are more common than you would think

Students with learning disabilities often struggle with developing and applying emotional intelligence skills, leading to difficulties in communication and academics. This can cause them to feel isolated and frustrated, affecting their academic and personal growth. While there are resources available to help students with learning disabilities, there is a lack of accessible resources specifically focused on developing emotional intelligence skills.

This is where The EQ Classroom comes in ...

Mission Statement

The EQ Classroom is an interactive web-based platform designed to enhance emotional intelligence among students with learning disabilities. Through engaging video lessons on typical social situations and ways to appropriately handle them tailored to the unique needs of students with learning disabilities, we empower students to build social and interpersonal skills.
